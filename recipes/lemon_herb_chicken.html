Lemon Herb Chicken

This is a recipe found in allrecipe website, by Emily Nienhaus Lemon herb chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup reduced sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 3 (10 to 12 ounces) chicken breasts, halved horizontally to get 6 cutlets
  • 1/2 tespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • (lemon wedges optional)

Steps

  1. Stir together chicken broth, garlic, heavy cream, Italian seasoning, and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a 4- to 5-quart slow cooker.
  2. Season chicken on both sides with remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium. Working in batches, sear half of the chicken until browned, 3 minutes each side.
  3. Remove chicken from skillet and place in slow cooker. Repeat with remaining oil and chicken. Cover and cook on low for 2 to 3 hours or until the chicken registers 165 degress F (74 degrees C) on an instant read thermometer.
  4. Using tongs, transfer chicken and garlic cloves to a serving platter.
  5. Whisk together lemon juice and cornstarch in a medium microwave-safe bowl. Add cooking liquid to the bowl; microwave for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes or until thickened, stirring every 30 seconds. Stir lemon zest into sauce.
  6. Serve chicken with lemon sauce and lemon wedges, if desired. Enjoy!
